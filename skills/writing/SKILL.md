## Writing Framework

Use this when the user asks for documentation, summaries, reports, copy, PRDs, emails, or rewrites.

1. Identify the audience and decision the writing supports.
2. Preserve facts and constraints from the source material.
3. Structure from most important to supporting detail.
4. Keep language plain and concrete.
5. Avoid filler, invented certainty, and over-broad claims.
6. Provide a concise final version unless the user requests exhaustive detail.
